One Day Trip by Shared Car in Lushun

2020-10-03 /
  • 生活
  • 随笔
 / 阅读耗时 3 分钟

Yestorday is the forth day of the National Day holiday, and it was a fine day. Four of us travelled to Lushun by shared car. Why did we travel to Lvshun? The reason is that one of us is from Lvshun , in this holiday he took us on a tour to his hometown. It’s about two hours’ journey from DUT to Lushun, I was thrilled all the way because this is my first time to Lvshun.

The first place in Lushun we went was Lushun Japan$Russia Prison Site, where is the necessary tourist attraction in Lushun. I’ve heard about it before, but I’ve never been here. My heart was heavy after the visit, we must bear in mind the history and the national humiliation.

The second place we went to Jiguan Mountain. Unfortunately, it is being renovated that tourists were not allowed to visit it, so we had to change out route to Baiyu Mountain. It’s allowed to drive to the top of the mountain, but there were so many tourists that it took us more than half an hour to get to the top of the mountain. At the top of the mountain, we can have a panoramic view of Lushun Port and the city. It is the best place to enjoy the panorama of Lushun city and the military port of Lushun.

After lunch, we went to the boundary between the Yellow Sea and the Bohai Sea, which is the southernmost tip of Liaodong Peninsula.

I thought I would see a clear line, but unfortunately I didn’t see it because of the weather.

After visiting here, we setted foot on the way to Dalian. Because it’s not too late after arriving in Dalian, we decided to visit the Xinghai Bay Bridge to enjoy the night view. It’s my regret that I didn’t take pictures here because of too many cars on the way.

It was a full day!
